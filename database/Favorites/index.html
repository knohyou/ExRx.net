Barbell Ben-over Row

d

Muscle: Back, General

Preparation: Bend knees slightly and bend over bar with back straight. Grasp bar with wide overhand grip. Execution: Pull bar to upper waist. Return until arms are extended and shoulders are stretched downward. Repeat.
